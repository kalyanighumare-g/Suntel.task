Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how the Library Management System collects, uses, and protects your personal information. By using our system, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Your name and contact details (e.g., email, phone number).
  • Library card details and membership information.
  • Details of books borrowed, returned, and reserved.
  • Any feedback or inquiries you submit through the system.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To manage your library account and track borrowed books.
  • To send notifications and reminders about due dates.
  • To improve the system based on user feedback.
  • To comply with legal and administrative requirements.

3. Information Protection

We are committed to ensuring the security of your information. We use industry-standard measures to prevent unauthorized access, data breaches, and misuse of your personal information.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law.
  • When you give us explicit consent.
  • To service providers who assist in system operations (bound by confidentiality agreements).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt-out of notifications and communication.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated via system notifications or email.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@librarysystem.com.
